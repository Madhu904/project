About Manali



Solang Valley


Solang Valley is a famous adventure and ski destination located near Manali. It offers activities like paragliding, skiing, zorbing, and ropeways, surrounded by snow-capped mountains and lush greenery.

Hadimba Devi Temple


The Hadimba Devi Temple, dedicated to Goddess Hadimba, is an ancient wooden temple built in 1553. Surrounded by cedar forests, it showcases beautiful Himachali architecture and holds cultural importance in Manali.
