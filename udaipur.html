About Udaipur



Udaipur City Palace


The City Palace of Udaipur is a magnificent royal residence on the banks of Lake Pichola. It features marble balconies, courtyards, and museums showcasing royal heritage and art of Mewar.

Lake Pichola


Lake Pichola is an artificial freshwater lake built in 1362. Surrounded by hills, palaces, and temples, it offers boat rides with mesmerizing sunset views and reflections of the City Palace.
