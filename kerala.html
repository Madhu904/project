ABOUT KERALA

Allepey

Alleppey, also known as Alappuzha, is famous for its backwaters and houseboat cruises.It offers a peaceful experience through canals lined with palm trees and traditional Kerala villages.

Munnar

Munnar is a serene hill station in Kerala known for its rolling tea gardens, misty hills, and pleasant weather. It's a paradise for nature lovers and honeymooners.
